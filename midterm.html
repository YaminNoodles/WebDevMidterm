Rush Hour 2

Summary

Days after the events of Rush Hour, Detective James Carter (Chris Tucker) of the LAPD is on vacation in Hong Kong with his partner Cheif Inspector Lee (Jackie Chan) of the Hong Kong Police Force after saving the Chinese Consul's daughter, Soo-Yung Han in the previous film. Soon after their arrival there was a package delivered to the US Embassy in Hong Kong where an explosion occcured killing two US custom agents that were investigating money laundering. Inspector Lee is assigned on this case to find who was behind the explosion. After discovering that Ricky Tan (John Lone), the former officer who was the partner of his late father is involved, it becomes personal. Ricky Tan is the leader of the Fu-Cang-Long Triad and sends his goons to make sure that Carter and Lee stray away from solving the case.
